# 🍓 Strawberry Orders

Fresh strawberry delivery for your apartment.

---

## Open the App

👉 **[Launch Strawberry Orders](https://scrapyduckstudio-strawbry-order.streamlit.app)**

---

## How it Works

### For Tenants
1. Open the app or scan the QR code on your door
2. Select products and quantities
3. Choose delivery time and payment method
4. Submit your order

### For Admin
1. Log in with admin credentials
2. View incoming orders in real-time (auto-refreshes every 15s)
3. Mark orders as delivered when done
4. Manage available products in the Stock tab

---

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🏠 Multi-apartment | 4 apartments with individual ordering |
| 🕐 Time slots | Flexible delivery windows |
| 💳 Payment options | Cash, Vipps, or Card |
| 📱 QR codes | Scan to auto-login, no password needed |
| 🔄 Live updates | Admin view refreshes automatically |
| 🛒 Stock control | Enable/disable products per day |

---

## Quick Access

- **Tenants**: Scan your apartment QR code or log in with your PIN
- **Admin**: Log in as `admin`
- **Super Admin**: Log in as `superadmin` for full control
